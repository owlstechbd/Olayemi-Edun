ABOUT ME

Hi There! I'm Olayemi Edun

Fashion Designer

Olayemi Edun is a Nigerian-born fashion designer based in Ireland, known for her unique illustration style and strong visual creativity. She began her fashion journey running a boutique in Lagos for over 10 years before relocating to Ireland to pursue formal studies in Fashion Design. A Top 10 Finalist in the Irish Young Designers’ Award 2018, Olayemi has refined her skills at Sallynoggin College and the National College of Art and Design (NCAD). Her work blends conceptual design, hand-drawn illustration, and a deep passion for creating garments that complement form, fabric, and individuality.
